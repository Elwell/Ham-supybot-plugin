This plugin is designed to give a few ham-radio related utilities to supybot
Main functions include:
 * QRZ <callsign> -- lookup callsign details on qrz.com
 * Sat <satellite> <location> -- gives prediction of next pass

Open to suggestions (and patches) for other functions.
Often tested by baconista on freenode in ##amateur-radio

Upstream devel on https://github.com/Elwell/Ham-supybot-plugin
